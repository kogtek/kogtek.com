About Kogtek

Seasoned Engineers, Fully in the Work

Kogtek was founded in 2017 by seasoned engineers who wanted to stay hands-on. We’ve spent years building production systems in healthcare, government, and fintech — and we’ve made AI-assisted development central to how we work, so we punch well above our size.

Our team covers Python, FastAPI, .NET, C#, TypeScript, Angular, AWS, Azure, GIS, and EHR integrations. We hold AWS certifications and have published research in machine learning and computer vision. We also bring backend engineering depth alongside a background in economics and audit — useful when the challenge isn’t purely technical.

We stay intentionally lean. When you work with Kogtek, you work with the people who scoped your project — from the first conversation through to deployment. No handoffs, no proposals handed to a different team, no surprises in who shows up to do the work.

Our Capabilities

What We Build

Senior-level engineering across backend systems, AI integration, and cloud — with domain depth in healthcare, government, and fintech.

Backend Engineering & APIs

We design and build reliable, scalable backend systems using Python/FastAPI and .NET/C#. From greenfield services to legacy modernization, we architect APIs that integrate cleanly, perform under load, and are built to be maintained — not just shipped and forgotten.

AI Integration & Automation

We integrate LLMs, computer vision, and ML models into real products — not demos. Whether you’re adding AI features to an existing system or building an AI-native application from scratch, we handle the architecture, the integrations, and the production deployment. We use AI tools in our own workflow, so we know where it genuinely earns its keep.

Cloud Architecture & DevOps

AWS and Azure, designed from first principles. We build and migrate cloud infrastructure, set up CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions and Jenkins, and architect for the scale you actually need. Our team includes AWS Certified Solutions Architects who have shipped cloud-native systems in production.

Healthcare Tech & EHR Integration

We have hands-on experience with EHR systems and FHIR standards, built across years of healthcare-focused backend work. If you’re building in healthcare and need engineers who already know the integration patterns, the compliance landscape, and the data models, we’ve done this before — at production scale.

Business Intelligence & Data

Raw data without insight is just storage cost. We design BI architectures and reporting layers that surface the metrics your team actually needs — from executive dashboards to operational drill-downs — connected to the data sources you already have.

GovTech & GIS Applications

We’ve built government-facing applications with geospatial capabilities using ArcGIS, .NET, and spatial data processing pipelines. If your project involves a map, public-sector workflows, or geospatial decision-support, we have the domain experience to navigate the constraints and deliver something that actually works in that environment.
